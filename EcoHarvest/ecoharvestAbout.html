About Us

Welcome To Eco Harvest

«ECO HARVEST» specializes in supplies of fresh natural fruits, berries, vegetables and greenery from Turkey, Azerbaijan and Kyrgyzstan to CIS and other countries. Our products are ecologically clean, grown in natural environment without usage of any chemicals and mineral fertilizers, due to which our fruits, berries and vegetables have specific natural taste. We have proven ourselves as a reliable supplier of high quality fresh natural products from Turkey, Azerbaijan and Kyrgyzstan. One of our company’s main principles is to ensure high quality of products we offer. We supply selected fruits, berries, vegetables and greenery, which pass careful calibration and quality inspection.

In Turkey, Azerbaijan and Kyrgyzstan we have our own contractual bases with modern cooler rooms, where we maintain optimal temperature for each cultivar of products, so that our greenery, vegetables, fruits and berries maintain their taste qualities, freshness and appearance. All of our products are delivered in refrigerated tracks, containers, as well as air cargo in a special temperature regime.

Nowadays, healthy nutrition demands for natural and eco-friendly products. Today you can make right choice and qualitatively change assortment of your products by buying ecologically clean fruits, berries, vegetables and greenery under the brand «ECO HARVEST»!

At ECO HARVEST, we deliver a whole world of freshness—no matter who, or where you are.
